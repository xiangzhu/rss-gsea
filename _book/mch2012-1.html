Integrated Enrichment Analysis of Genetic Variants and Biological Pathways using GWAS Summary Data

25.1 GSEA Round 1

Last updated: 2016-09-13

Code version: f965d5eb3b425d712c3e7c14c3704300c8299dbe

25.1.1 Fit the null model

I used the grids theta0=(-5:0.25:-2)' and h=(0.3:0.1:0.6)' when fitting the null model. I estimated the (unnormalized) log importance weights (“pseudo-likelihoods”) for all 52 sets of (h,theta0) under null.

After normalizing the log “pseudo-likelihoods” (logw.step*) to posterior probabilities (posp.step*), we can see that almost 100% posterior mass is concentrated on the setting h=0.6 and theta0=-4.

25.1.2 Fit the enrichment model
