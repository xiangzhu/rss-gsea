15.1 GSEA Round 1

Last updated: 2016-06-01

Code version: 6c8000abc2ae35c236bea82c20de432143c1d494

15.1.1 Fit the null model

I used the grids theta0=(-4:0.25:-2)' and h=(0.3:0.1:0.6)' when fitting the null model. I estimated the (unnormalized) log importance weights (“pseudo-likelihoods”) for all 36 sets of (h,theta0) under null.

After normalizing the log “pseudo-likelihoods” (logw.step*) to posterior probabilities (posp.step*), we can see that almost 100% posterior mass is concentrated on the setting (h=0.3, theta0=-3).

15.1.2 Fit the enrichment model
